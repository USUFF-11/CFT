Frequently Asked Questions

What documents are required for export/import?

+

Typical export/import documentation includes commercial invoice, packing list, bill of lading/airway bill, certificate of origin, and any required permits or licenses. The specific documents required depend on the product and destination country.

What are your payment terms?

+

We offer various payment terms including advance payment, letter of credit (L/C), and open account (for established customers). Our standard terms are 30% deposit with order and 70% before shipment for new customers.

How do you handle shipping and logistics?

+

We have established relationships with major shipping lines and logistics providers worldwide. We handle all aspects of shipping including documentation, customs clearance, and door-to-door delivery if required. We can ship via air, sea, or land based on your requirements.

What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

+

MOQ varies by product. For most products, we can accommodate small trial orders, while bulk orders typically receive better pricing. Please contact us with your specific requirements for a customized quote.

Do you provide product samples?

+

Yes, we can provide samples for most products. Sample costs and shipping fees apply, which may be credited against future orders. Contact us for sample availability and pricing.
